About us

A Tradition
of Excellence

For over twenty years we have crafted furniture that goes beyond function. Every element leaves our atelier made from the finest materials — hand-selected exotic timbers, noble leathers and precision-finished metals.

We work exclusively with private clients and acclaimed interior design studios. Our pieces reside in residences across London, Warsaw, Geneva and Dubai.

How We Work

Four steps
to an exceptional piece

01

Consultation

We meet at the atelier or remotely. We listen and understand your space. No commitment, no pressure.

02

Design

3D visualisations, material selection, iterations. You approve the design personally — at your own pace.

03

Production

Hand-crafted throughout. Every element quality-checked. 8–16 weeks depending on complexity.
