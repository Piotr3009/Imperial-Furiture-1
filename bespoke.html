The Bespoke Process

How a bespoke
commission comes to life

01

Initial Meeting

We meet at the atelier or on site. We listen to your vision, study the space and understand your way of living. No catalogue, no pressure.

02

Design & Proposal

Our designers produce hand-drawn sketches and 3D renders. We present material samples in person. You iterate freely until every detail is right.

03

Fixed Quote

Once the design is approved, you receive a fixed price and timeline. Nothing changes from this point without your written consent.

04

Workshop Production

Your piece is built entirely by hand in our London workshop. We photograph and document each stage. You are welcome to visit at any point.

05

Delivery & Install

We deliver and install personally. We do not use couriers or third-party installers. The piece arrives in white-glove condition, every time.
